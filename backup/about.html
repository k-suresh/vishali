About us

As the saying goes “Even the most distant dream can be realized with determination and persistence”, we had a dream to contribute and to pay back to the mother country. We have a dream to wipe poverty from our country. We all if worked together can achieve this and that is how our journey begun and the primary focus were shot at education.

Vishali foundation are a group of like minded people who are determined to be a force to reckon with, who have persistence to put a long stride forward to support our poor brothers and sisters.

The family of Vishali which consists of members, donors, volunteers and students constantly thrive to make a difference, a huge difference in the lives of children who live in poverty and yet dream to be good educated citizens.
